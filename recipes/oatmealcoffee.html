OATMEALCOFFEE RECIPE

INGRIDIENTS

in this recipe you will learn how to make a delicious sludgy oatmeal coffee

you need this ingridients to acomplish best oatmeal coffee in the know world

  • oatmeal(seeds and powder)
  • coffee
  • sugar

HOW TO

follow this steps

  1. put coffee on stove
  2. put the powder and oatmeal seeds in a cup of water
  3. mix it on a blender
  4. add coffee with sugar and mix again
  5. enjoy best OATMEALCOFFEE in the known world

VENEKOHAWTDOGS RECIPE

in this recipe venekohawtdogs will blow your head

INGRIDIENTS

you need this ingridients for maximum potential

  • buns
  • sausages
  • crispy potatoes
  • onions
  • cabbage
  • various types of sausages

HOW TO

follow this steps

  1. put sausages in a saucepan and boil 'em
  2. wait until the sausages are cooked and then put them on the bun
  3. chop the onion into small bits and sprinkle it over the hot dog with the crispy potatoes
  4. throw all kinds of sauces to your taste, ideally: ketchup, mustard, mayonise, etc.
  5. enjoy the grotesque
